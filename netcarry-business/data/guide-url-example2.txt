<div class="clearer"></div><h2 id="Section_Background_and_Identification">Background and Identification<a class="anchor" href="#Section_Background_and_Identification" title="链接到该区域">&nbsp;&para;&nbsp;</a></h2>

<p>The Fire Phone is Amazon's first foray into the smartphone market. The device boasts a 2.2 GHZ quad-core processor, 2 GB of RAM, 4.7&quot; 720P IPS display, and runs Fire OS 3.5.1, a custom operating system based on Android 4.2.2 (Jelly Bean).</p>

<p>The Fire Phone features &quot;Dynamic Perspective&quot;, a 3D display technology that uses four front-facing infrared cameras to create a sort of &quot;depth&quot; to the UI. It also features &quot;Firefly&quot;, an app that uses various recognition technologies to identify over 100 million products, songs, and movies. Additionally, the Fire Phone comes packed with &quot;Mayday&quot;, Amazon's 24/7 tech support feature which was first released on the Kindle Fire HDX line of tablets.</p>

<div class="clearer"></div><h2 id="Section_Technical_Specifications">Technical Specifications<a class="anchor" href="#Section_Technical_Specifications" title="链接到该区域">&nbsp;&para;&nbsp;</a></h2>

<p><strong>Release date:</strong> July 24, 2014</p>

<p><strong>Developer:</strong> Amazon</p>

<p><strong>Operating System:</strong> Fire OS 3.5.1, based on Android 4.2.2</p>

<p><strong>Display:</strong> 4.7&quot; 1280 × 720 (315 PPI) IPS LCD touchscreen</p>

<p><strong>CPU:</strong> 2.2 GHz quad-core Snapdragon 800</p>

<p><strong>GPU:</strong> 450 MHz Adreno 330</p>

<p><strong>RAM:</strong> 2 GB</p>

<p><strong>Storage:</strong> 32/64 GB Flash Memory</p>

<p><strong>Communications:</strong> Single-stream 802.11b/g/n/ac, Bluetooth 3.0, NFC</p>

<p><strong>Network:</strong></p>

<ul><li>UMTS/HSPA+/DC-HSDPA (850, 900, 1700/2100, 1900, 2100 MHz)</li><li>Quad-band GSM/EDGE (850, 900, 1800, 1900 MHz)</li><li>9 4G-LTE bands (Bands 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 7, 8, 17, 20)</li></ul>

<p><strong>Cameras:</strong></p>

<ul><li>13MP rear f/2.0 camera with OIS</li><li>2.1 MP front camera</li></ul>

<p><strong>Ports:</strong></p>

<ul><li>Micro USB 2.0</li><li>3.5mm headphone jack</li></ul>

<p><strong>Dimensions:</strong> 5.5&quot; x 2.6&quot; x 0.35&quot; (139.2 x 66.5 x 8.9 mm)</p>

<p><strong>Weight:</strong> 5.64 ounces (160 g)</p>

<p><strong>Battery:</strong> 2400 mAh capacity</p>
